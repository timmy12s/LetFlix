About LeTflix


LeTflix. Inc. is an American content platform production company headquartered in Los Gatos, California. LeTflix was founded in 2021 by Stefanus Hermawan and his friends in Los Gatos, California. The company’s primary business is a subscription based streaming service offering online streaming from a library of films and television series, including those produced in house.


Our goal is to make movie as the mood booster from everyone in the world. When people gets sad or upset about something or just want to boost their mood they can just watch our movies. By working as a team we value our co-workers as our family and we keep helping each other to make our working space as a better place to work in.

LeTflix History

2018

Letflix was built by Stefanus Hermawan and 2 of his friends, Vincent, and Timmy in 2018. Back then, they were a Computer Science student in one of the biggest University in Indonesia called Binus University and were doing side project to be made as our portfolio.

2019

In 2019, LeTflix made a partnership with other market agent called BoBa marketing to help LeTflix grow and made it to the big scene of streaming services in the world.

2020

LeTflix was made public and open for public use for the first time and made a new world of record for Most Streamed Website in 1 Week after publishing.

2021

Letflix made an initial public offering with market cap of 10 Billion USD at the local stock in Indonesia with the growth about 300% in just 1 month after the intial public offering.
